Hattrick

The online soccer manager game.

How to win

Hattrick is an online soccer strategy game where you build and manage an imaginary soccer team for a long-term run.
You receive a pre-populated team of mostly junk players and lead that team to the top division of your country.
To do so, you need to do the following:

  1. Establish a good coaching network
  2. Create a youth academy
  3. Maintain a stadium that will generate enough revenue
  4. Intelligent purchasing and selling of players in the transfer market.
